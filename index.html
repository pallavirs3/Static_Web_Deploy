Pallavi's Strength Training Plan

Your 6-day power-packed workout routine to build strength and confidence

"Strength doesn't come from what you can do. It comes from overcoming the things you once thought you couldn't."
- Rikki Rogers
Day 1: Upper Body Power

Bench Press 4x5

Focus on explosive push from chest

  • Warm-up: 2 sets of 8 with light weight
  • Working sets: Increase weight each set
  • Rest: 2-3 minutes between sets

Pull-Ups 4x6-8

Full range of motion

  • Use assistance if needed
  • Focus on controlled movement

Overhead Press 3x8

Strict form, no leg drive

Day 2: Lower Body Power

Back Squat 4x5

Below parallel, explosive up

  • Warm-up thoroughly
  • Increase weight each set
  • Brace core throughout

Romanian Deadlift 3x8

Hamstring focus

Bulgarian Split Squat 3x10/leg

Controlled tempo

Day 3: Active Recovery

Mobility Work 30 min

Focus on tight areas

Core Circuit 3 rounds

  • Plank - 60 sec
  • Hanging Leg Raises - 12 reps
  • Russian Twists - 20/side

Light Cardio 20 min

Walking, cycling, or swimming

Day 4: Upper Body Hypertrophy

Incline Dumbbell Press 4x8-10

Upper chest focus

Lat Pulldown 4x10-12

Full stretch at top

Dumbbell Shoulder Press 3x10

Biceps & Triceps 3x12 each

Superset for efficiency

Day 5: Lower Body Hypertrophy

Front Squat 4x8

Upright torso, elbows high

Hip Thrust 4x12

Glute activation

Leg Curl 3x12

Slow eccentric

Calf Raises 4x15

Full range of motion

Day 6: Full Body Conditioning

Circuit Training 4 rounds

  • Kettlebell Swings - 15 reps
  • Burpees - 10 reps
  • Battle Ropes - 30 sec
  • Box Jumps - 8 reps
  • Rest 1 min between rounds

Core Finisher 3 rounds

  • Hanging Knee Raises - 12 reps
  • Ab Wheel Rollouts - 10 reps
  • Side Plank - 30 sec/side
